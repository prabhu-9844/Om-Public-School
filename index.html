About Us

Welcome to OM Public School

OM Public School, founded in 2005 by Ms. Rukmini Sawkar, an Agriculturist, Politician, and Social Activist, embodies a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality education accessible to all, regardless of their location or background. Situated in Haveri District, the school has been a beacon of learning, serving both rural and urban communities. Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, OM Public School remained committed to its mission. Through innovative measures and the dedication of its highly qualified faculty, the school seamlessly transitioned to online learning, ensuring that education continued uninterrupted. This resilience and adaptability reflect the school's unwavering commitment to providing quality education, even in the face of adversity.
